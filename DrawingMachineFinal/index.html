Draw Your Own Bouquet

Flowers That Will Always Last Forever ~

Statement

Continuing with the genre of “quarantine art” in this project, I decided to create a drawing machine where the user can draw a bouquet and send it to someone special. With the ongoing continuation of social distancing, many of us are unable to celebrate or comfort loved ones. However, with this drawing machine, one can draw a bouquet to send to a loved one virtually. No contact is required! I was inspired by looking at the colorful scribbles on old cards that I made as a kid for different occasions. These handmade-cards are much more personal and sentimental compared to premade cards. I wanted to recreate the same sentiment in this drawing machine, allowing the user to create a bouquet that reflects their emotions to the recipient. The flowers the user draws can last forever, unlike real flowers.

Instructions

  • click 'C' to clear the canvas
  • hit 'S' to save image as a PNG.
  • click 'R' to add RED to the color of your stroke.
  • click 'G' to add GREEN to the color of your stroke.
  • click 'B' to add BLUE to the color of your stroke.
  • click 'A' to make the color of your stroke darker.
  • click 'W' to subtract RED from the color of your stroke.
  • click 'D' to subtract GREEN from the color of your stroke.
  • click 'V' to subtract BLUE from the color of your stroke.
  • click 'F' to make the color of your stroke lighter.
  • hit 'O' to create circle outlines on the canvas.
  • hit 'U' to add rings to your stroke.
  • The slider under the canvas controls the size of your stroke.
  • After you have saved the canvas, use the box on right to type in the name of the person(s) that you are sending the bouquet to!
